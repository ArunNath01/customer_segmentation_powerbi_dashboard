# Customer Segmentation Power BI Dashboard

## Overview

The **Customer Segmentation Power BI Dashboard** is an interactive business intelligence solution built using **Microsoft Power BI** to analyze customer performance, revenue, profitability, regional trends, and product insights. This project uses a **dummy dataset** for demonstration and portfolio purposes, showcasing how Power BI transforms raw business data into actionable insights.

The dashboard consists of **three interactive report pages**:

1. Executive Overview
2. Customer Segmentation Analysis
3. Regional & Product Insights

---

## Objectives

- Monitor overall business performance using key performance indicators (KPIs).
- Analyze customer segments based on revenue and profitability.
- Identify customer purchasing preferences.
- Compare regional performance.
- Discover top-performing products and categories.
- Support data-driven decision-making through interactive visualizations.

# Dashboard Pages

## 1. Executive Overview

Provides a high-level summary of overall business performance.

### KPI Cards

- Total Revenue
- Total Profit
- Profit Margin
- Total Transactions

### Visualizations

-  **Line Chart** – Revenue Trend Over Time
-  **Line Chart** – Profit Trend Over Time
-  **Donut Chart** – Revenue Contribution by Customer Type
-  **Bar Chart** – Total Profit by Customer Type

### Insights

- Monitor business growth over time.
- Track profitability trends.
- Identify customer segments contributing the highest revenue.
- Compare profits across customer types.

---

## 2. Customer Segmentation Analysis

Focuses on customer behavior and purchasing patterns.

### Visualizations

-  **Clustered Bar Chart** – Revenue vs Profit by Customer Type
-  **Clustered Bar Chart** – Product Preference by Customer Type
-  **Treemap** – Customer Type Revenue Share

### Insights

- Compare revenue and profit among customer segments.
- Analyze product preferences across customer types.
- Identify customer groups generating the highest revenue.

---

## 3. Regional & Product Insights

Provides geographical and product-level performance analysis.

### Visualizations

-  **Bar Chart** – Total Profit by Region
-  **Bar Chart** – Top 10 Products by Profit
-  **Table** – Category Performance Summary
-  **Treemap** – Revenue by State

### Insights

- Compare profitability across regions.
- Identify the most profitable products.
- Evaluate category performance.
- Analyze state-wise revenue contribution.
